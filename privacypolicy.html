Privacy Policy for AI Companion

1. Data Collection

AI Companion does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) without your consent. The Extension may access specific data solely to provide its core functionality and improve your user experience.

Types of Data Collected:

  • Usage Data: Certain usage data (e.g., feature usage statistics, error reports) may be collected to enhance the Extension’s performance. This data is anonymized and not linked to you personally.
  • Speech Data _(If applicable)_: If using speech-to-text or similar features, audio data may be processed temporarily on your device but is not saved or transmitted.

2. Use of Data

Any data collected is used solely to:

  • Improve and optimize the Extension's performance.
  • Provide core functionalities as advertised.
  • Ensure compliance with security standards.

We do not share, sell, or lease any collected data to third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

The Extension may integrate with third-party APIs or services (e.g., for speech-to-text, analytics, or cloud services). These services may collect data in accordance with their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these services before enabling them in the Extension.

4. Data Security

We take data security seriously and employ industry-standard practices to safeguard data, such as anonymizing collected data and using encrypted channels for data transfers when necessary.

5. Your Rights

Since we do not collect or retain personal data, there is no data to access, correct, or delete. Should this change in the future, users will be notified and granted the right to request data access, correction, or deletion as per legal requirements.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. If significant changes are made, we will notify users through the Extension or by updating the “Last updated” date.

7. Contact Us
